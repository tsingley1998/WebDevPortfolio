Where Have I been?

I haven't actually been many places, but these are just a few of my favorite places in the world. In my list of my favorite sites, I've included several links to sources that can tell you more about each place. Enjoy!

  1. Philadelphia, Pennsylvania- My dad grew up here, and my Grammie still lives there. I love to go and visit her, as well as see the many historical sites it has to offer.
  2. Bentonville, Arkansas- I had the opportunity to serve a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Bentonville. I met so many amazing people and made the best memories there.
  3. Victorville, California- I love Victorville because that is where Spencer and his family live. It's also in Southern California which means minimal snow!
  4. New York City, New York- I actually hated New York City, but I included it because when we visited, we saw Wicked on Broadway. That was always a dream of mine. It met every expectation too, but now that it is fulfilled, I wouldn't mind if I
    just never went back.
